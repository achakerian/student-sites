Hazelnut Chocolate Meringue Cake

Est. 1935

Ingredients:

  • 6 large egg whites
  • 300g caster sugar
  • 150g toasted hazelnuts, chopped
  • 100g dark chocolate, melted
  • 50g mini chocolate chips
  • 500ml heavy cream
  • 50g icing sugar
  • Dark chocolate shavings for garnish

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 110°C. Line 3 large baking sheets with parchment paper, drawing 20cm circles as guides.
2. Toast hazelnuts at 180°C for 8-10 minutes until golden. Cool completely, then roughly chop.
3. In a spotlessly clean bowl, whisk egg whites to soft peaks. Gradually add caster sugar, 1 tablespoon at a time, whisking well after each addition until thick and glossy.
4. Gently fold in chopped hazelnuts and chocolate chips. Drizzle melted chocolate over mixture and marble through with a knife.
5. Divide mixture between prepared baking sheets, spreading within the circles. Bake for 2.5 hours until crisp and easily lifted from parchment.
6. Cool completely on trays. Whip cream with icing sugar to soft peaks. Layer meringue discs with cream, finishing with chocolate shavings. Chill 2 hours before serving.
